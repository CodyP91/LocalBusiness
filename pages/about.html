About TECHQUAL

TECHQUAL is a technology-focused business that was founded by John and Susan in 2010. John and Susan are both tech enthusiasts who wanted to bring the latest and greatest technology products to their community. They believed that everyone should have access to high-quality technology products and services, regardless of their budget.

John and Susan started small, working out of a small storefront in downtown. They would spend hours researching the latest technology trends, sourcing the best products, and providing top-notch customer service to their clients. Word quickly spread about their expertise and dedication, and before they knew it, they had a loyal following of customers.

Over the years, TECHQUAL has grown and expanded, but they have never lost sight of their commitment to quality and affordability. Today, their product range includes a wide variety of technology products, including computers, smartphones, tablets, and accessories. They also offer a range of services, including repairs, installations, and consultations.

Meet the Owners

John is a self-taught tech guru who has been passionate about technology since he was a child. He loves nothing more than researching the latest trends and finding ways to help his customers get the most out of their technology products.

Susan is a marketing expert who has been instrumental in growing Ok Pizza into the successful business it is today. She is always looking for new ways to reach out to customers and promote the latest products and services.
